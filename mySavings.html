mySavings
Be rewarded with higher interest when you save more each month. Open your account today!

How it works

  1. Open an mySavings Account via your sembank app.
  2. Set aside a monthly savings amount between S$50 to S$3,000 (in multiplies of S$10) from your existing semBank Savings or Current account. This designated amount will be credited into your mySavings account.
  3. Select your preferred debiting date between the 1st and 25th of each month. Ensure that you have enough funds in your source account by 7pm, one day before the debiting date.
  4. Earn up to 0.25% p.a. interest on your savings.
  5. Easily amend the monthly savings amount and debiting date via sembank

Eligibility & Fees

  • Minimum age:16 years old
  • Eligibility: To apply for a personal mySavings account, you must have an existing sembank Savings or Current Account.
  • Fee: No Service charge & initial deposit
